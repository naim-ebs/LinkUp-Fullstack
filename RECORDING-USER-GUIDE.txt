Q: Can other participants see I'm recording?
A: Yes! The REC indicator is visible to you only, but it's good
   practice to inform participants.

Q: Where are recordings stored?
A: In your browser's download folder. They are NOT uploaded anywhere.

Q: Can I pause recording?
A: Not currently. Stop and start a new recording if needed.

Q: What if I leave the meeting while recording?
A: Recording automatically stops and downloads.

Q: Can I record without video?
A: Recording needs at least one video stream to work.

Q: How long can I record?
A: As long as you have disk space! No time limit.

Q: Can multiple people record the same meeting?
A: Yes! Each person can record independently.

Q: Are recordings encrypted?
A: The download is a plain .webm file. Encrypt it yourself if needed.
